### AI Summary
This amendment to the Stamp Duties (Section 23) Order 2017 changes the holding period requirement for equity interests acquired on or after 4 July 2025, extending it from 3 years to 4 years, while maintaining the 3-year period for interests acquired between 11 March 2017 and 3 July 2025.
