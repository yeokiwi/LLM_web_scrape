### AI Summary
This order grants Ascott REIT (Europe) Pte. Ltd. a tax exemption for interest income received from The Ascott (Europe) N.V. and Ascott Holdings (France) SAS starting 4 July 2025, specifically income derived from rental, property-related earnings, and capital gains from the divestment of "La Clef Tour Eiffel Paris" in France, subject to conditions outlined in a letter from the Inland Revenue Authority of Singapore dated 4 July 2025.
