### AI Summary
This amendment order modifies the Town Councils (Declaration) Order 2025 by inserting the phrase "the eve of" after "earlier than" in both paragraph 10(3)(b) and paragraph 11(2)(b), effectively changing the timing reference for specified actions from occurring earlier than a given date to occurring earlier than the eve of that date.
